Isabel Rowbotham, Co-Editor-in-Chief

Isabel is a science writer covering topics on human health affected by climate change and pollution. She moved from Venezuela in 2013 and has a background in Cellular and Molecular Medicine (BSc) and Cardiovascular Medicine (MSc). She has previous experience writing for the University of Bristol newspaper, Epigram on SciTech topics including COVID-19, research involving Big Data and data sharing, and the NHS.
